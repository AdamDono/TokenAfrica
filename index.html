About Us

Nero is Africa’s first Hyper Deflationary token on the binance blockchain, with a low supply that will keep on decreasing till the end of time.


Nero is a native token to NeroSwap which will be africa’s first Decentralized wallet to have a decentralized swap an african Nft market place.

Our aim is to empower your portfolio effectively, whilst challenging the status quo and bridging the gap between African investors and good, trustful and low cap tokens.
